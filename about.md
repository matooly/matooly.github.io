Hi, I'm Matin. 
I write code, train models, and overanalyze everything else.
I spend a significant amount of my time working in Python, Java, and Go, whether I am extracting insights from financial platforms or exploring the theoretical mathematical foundations behind neural networks. I enjoy building things that think, scale, and solve complex problems.

However, a strict diet of logic and algorithms isn't enough.
I built this space to document everything else. Beyond data structures and cloud architecture, I am deeply interested in character-driven cinema, the quiet mysteries of the night sky, and historical letters written by people who wanted to set the world on fire. Consider this platform somewhere I yap about things i like.

So I try to keep this place not all technical.
